## 2. Principle: Minimal Data Processing

Travel Delta does not require user accounts and does not process personal data such as names, email addresses, or location. The app is designed to operate with minimal data collection.

## 3. Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored exclusively on your device (SharedPreferences) and is not transmitted to third parties:

- Selected countries (home country and destination)
- Saved routes
- Preferences (language, appearance)
- Cached travel data (exchange rates, entry requirements, emergency numbers)

This data remains entirely on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.

## 4. External Services and Network Requests

The following external services are used to operate the app:

**Supabase** (Supabase Inc., San Francisco, USA)
Loads travel data such as entry requirements, emergency numbers, and roaming information. Only country codes are transmitted (e.g. "DE", "US"). No personal data is transferred. Data is cached locally for up to 24 hours. More info: [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

**frankfurter.app** (public exchange rate API)
Retrieves currency exchange rates. Only currency codes are transmitted (e.g. "EUR", "USD"). No personal data is involved. Rates are cached locally for up to 4 hours.

**Apple App Store / Google Play Store**
For premium purchases, Apple or Google handle payment processing under their own privacy policies. Travel Delta receives only an anonymised confirmation of purchase status.

## 5. Legal Basis

Processing is based on Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract performance, where a premium feature is used), Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in operating the app), and Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent, where required).

## 6. No Analytics or Tracking

Travel Delta does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. No usage profiles are created.

## 7. International Data Transfers

Supabase may process data on servers in the USA. Supabase Inc. is certified under the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework. Only non-personal country codes are transmitted.

## 8. Your Rights (Art. 15–22 GDPR)

You have the right to:

- Access your stored data (Art. 15)
- Rectification of inaccurate data (Art. 16)
- Erasure (Art. 17)
- Restriction of processing (Art. 18)
- Data portability (Art. 20)
- Objection to processing (Art. 21)

To exercise your rights, contact: benedikt.schreiner@googlemail.com

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.
